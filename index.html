Our Business

Cotton is considered the ‘king of crops’ and is native to tropical and subtropical regions around the world, including the Americas, Africa, and India.. The major cotton producing countries are USA, China, India, Pakistan, Uzbekistan, Egypt, Argentina, Australia, Greece, Brazil and Turkey. These countries contribute about 85% to the global Cotton production. Cotton is the most important fiber crop of the world and it is also one of the most widely grown commercial crops in Pakistan.

Pakistan is the fifth largest producer of cotton in the world, the third largest exporter of raw cotton, the fourth largest consumer of cotton, and the largest exporter of cotton yarn. 1.3 million farmers (out of a total of 5 million) cultivate cotton over 3 million hectares, covering 15 per cent of the cultivable area in the country.

About Us

Rehman Cotton Company is a leading "family-owned company" . It works through two subsitiry companies dealing with different fields of our business.

  • Rehman Cotton Ginners
  • Rehman Traders

Rehman Cotton Company started its business back in 1997-1998 crop season in south Punjab, which is well known for huge cultivation of cotton crop in Pakistan. Our first enterprise Rehman Traders delt with the needs of brokerage and trading of lint & cotton seed, it has grown throughout steadily and has become leading brokerage house in Pakistan.

In December 2021, merger of Bismillah Cotton Company & Rehman Cotton Company took place and sharing of top-level expertise, brought new vision and destinations to our already established business. "Now, we are all set to explore international markets to buy and sell cotton related products."

We stepped into production in crop year 2009-2010 and developed own cotton ginning plant as Rehman Cotton Ginners, purpose was production of special cotton as per demand of our spinners. Newly cultivated land of Balochistan, owing to its virginity and a very suitable weather, provide best quality raw cotton to ginners in our area. Our passion for cotton keeps us motivated to buy extra special raw cotton. With an untiring care and expert handling, we convert it into top quality lint for our very special customers.
A big number of satisfied customers is a proof of rapid and stable growth of our ginning business.
